Frequently Asked

The honest answers.

If your question isn't here, our team will answer it on the phone or in your consultation — no scripts, no upsell.

Call us directly +1 (310) 555-0142
Do you accept insurance?

We are out-of-network with all PPO plans, which means we work for you, not the insurer. Our team files all paperwork on your behalf, and most patients receive 40–70% reimbursement directly from their insurance company.

What does a consultation cost?

The initial 60-minute consultation, including digital scan and 3D photography, is $295. The fee is credited toward your treatment if you choose to proceed.

How long do veneers actually last?

Our porcelain veneers carry a 10-year warranty, but in practice most last 15–20 years or longer with normal care. We use feldspathic and lithium-disilicate porcelains — the materials with the longest documented clinical track record.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. We partner with CareCredit and Lending Club for treatment financing, with 0% APR plans available for qualified applicants up to 24 months. Our treatment coordinator will walk you through every option at consultation.

I'm anxious about the dentist. How do you handle that?

About a third of our patients describe themselves as anxious. We offer nitrous, oral sedation, and IV sedation administered by a board-certified anesthesiologist — plus weighted blankets, noise-cancelling headphones, and unhurried appointments by default.

Can I see a digital preview of my smile before treatment?

Always. Every cosmetic case includes a digital smile design plus a removable wax mockup you can wear home for a weekend. No treatment begins until you have approved the final shape and shade.
